# Privacy Policy

PinPilot is a personal, single-user tool for creating and scheduling Pinterest pins with Amazon affiliate links. It is operated by its owner for their own use.

**What we store.** Your email address to sign you in, the products and pins you create, your posting schedule, settings, and — if you connect Pinterest — access tokens that let the app post pins to your own Pinterest account. Tokens are encrypted at rest. If you import Amazon earnings reports, their contents are stored to show your earnings dashboard.

**Pinterest data.** With your permission, the app reads your boards, trending topics, and the performance of pins it created, and creates pins on your behalf. It never reads private data of other Pinterest users and never posts anywhere you have not chosen.

**What we never do.** No selling data, no advertising, no analytics trackers, no sharing with third parties beyond the services the app exists to use: Pinterest, Amazon, and the email provider that sends sign-in links.

**Deleting your data.** Disconnecting Pinterest removes its tokens immediately. Deleting the app database removes everything else.
